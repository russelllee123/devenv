Benching cues:

In order to perform a proper bench press, engage your core and lock your feet in place on the ground, tuck your lats under your back,lift the barbell from the rack, breathe in while dropping the barbell to the middle of your chest, and simultaneously exhale, drive with your legs, and push the barbell off of your chest and until your arms lock out.
