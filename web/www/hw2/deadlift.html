Deadlifting cues:

In order to perform a proper bench deadlift, put your feet at shoulder-width with the middle of your feet under the barbell, grab the barbell just outside of your foot width, breathe in, engage your core, drop your glutes to the point where your upper body is at a 15 degree angle relative to the ground, and drive simultaneously with your legs and glutes until you are standing upright holding the barbell.
